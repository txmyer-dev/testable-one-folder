# The "Fractional IT" Sales Pitch

## Target: The Overwhelmed Business Owner (25-50 Employees)

### The Hook (The Problem)
"You're big enough that IT is breaking every day, but too small to spend $70k on a full-time IT Manager. You're currently 'getting by' with a local computer guy who bills you hourly, which means he only makes money when your business is broken."

### The Solution (The Fractional Model)
"I offer a **Fractional IT Department**. For a flat **$750/week**, I provide the same tools and security as a Fortune 500 company. 

Because it’s a flat rate, **my incentives are aligned with yours.** I make the most money when your systems never break. So, I spend my time automating your security and maintenance so you never have to call me."

### The "Three Pillars" Close
1.  **Syncro Automation:** "I use enterprise software to patch your systems and fix issues in the background before your staff even notices."
2.  **Huntress SOC:** "I provide a 24/7 Security Operations Center. Real humans watch your network while we sleep. If a threat hits at 2 AM, it's isolated before your first employee walks in at 8 AM."
3.  **Predictable Billing:** "No more 'surprise' $5,000 invoices because a server went down. It's $750 every Monday. Period."

### Handling the "Why so cheap?" Objection
"I don't have the overhead of a 50-person MSP with a fancy office and a fleet of vans. I use better technology (Syncro/Huntress) to do more work in less time. You’re paying for the result—a working business—not for my commute time."
