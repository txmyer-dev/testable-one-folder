# Offerings & Pricing (The 50-User Model)

## The Core Tier: The Fractional IT Department
**Price:** $750/Week (Billed Mondays) or $3,200/Month

### The 50-User Value Proposition
For a 50-person company, this model replaces a $60,000/year internal IT hire with a $38,400/year professional department, saving them over **$20,000/year** while increasing security.

### What’s Included:
*   **24/7 SOC Protection:** Real-time threat hunting via **Huntress**.
*   **Unlimited Remote Support:** 9am–5pm response for all 50 users.
*   **Automated Maintenance:** All Windows and 3rd-party patches handled via **Syncro**.
*   **Weekly Value Report:** Automated "Health Score" delivered every Friday.
*   **M365 Security:** Continuous monitoring of company email accounts for hacks.

### The "Standard Fleet" Requirement:
To qualify for the flat $750/week rate, the client must meet these standards:
*   Workstations must be Windows 10/11 or macOS 12+.
*   All hardware must be under 5 years old (or on a scheduled replacement plan).
*   Standardization on M365 or Google Workspace.
